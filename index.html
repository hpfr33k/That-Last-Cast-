App Features

  • Offline loadout predictions based on location and species
  • Smart tackle box with labeled photos and item tracking
  • Music capture from Spotify, YouTube Music, etc.
  • Fish length estimator using rod and camera
  • Legacy album for family fishing memories
  • Integrated maps with lake pins, GPS tagging, and Google Maps layers
  • Toggleable features with presets like "Tournament Mode"
